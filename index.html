The Universal Draft Lottery Simulator

Calculate the odds and simulate an NBA/MLB/NHL-like draft lottery with any number of teams and any probabilities you want. You have complete flexibility in how the lottery is generated and performed. Use it for your fantasy league, or just for playing around.
If you like simulating hypothetical draft lotteries, maybe you'd like simulating a whole league? ZenGM has you covered! Play basketball, football, baseball, or hockey. You can customize the draft lottery and tons of other things, and play as many seasons as you want. All for free!
